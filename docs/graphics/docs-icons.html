Icons

400 exclusive icons, made for Replacer

How to use

We made 400 line icons, especially for Replacer, so feel free to customize them in the Figma file, which is also included and look at how to use them in your markups:

PugHTML
.icon.bg-primary.rounded-lg.p-2.mr-2
	img(alt='Icon', src='/images/icons/white/cursor-click-2.svg', height='48')
.icon.bg-primary-light.rounded-lg.p-2.mr-2
	img(alt='Icon', src='/images/icons/primary/layers-3.svg', height='48')
.icon.bg-secondary-light.rounded-circle.p-2.mr-2
	img(alt='Icon', src='/images/icons/secondary/mac.svg', height='48')
<div class="icon bg-primary rounded-lg p-2 mr-2">
	<img alt="Icon" src="/images/icons/white/cursor-click-2.svg" height="48px">
</div>
