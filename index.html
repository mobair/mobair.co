Providing
supply air

We specialize in designing supply air valves for improving the ventilation and quality of the air inside homes and buildings. Our products are room-specific and are designed to make the existing ventilation, natural or mechanical, more efficient.

Supply Air ValvesFind Resellers
Simple Installation

Valves can be installed directly onto the wall, e.g. on top of the opening of a fresh air duct or a specially made ventilation opening. Fully compatible with the building's existing ventilation.

Efficient

The energy consumption of a room-specific ventilation is only a fraction compared to a traditional centralized ventilation system. Pre-heated supply air lessens the workload of other heating sources.

Affordable

The cost of a room-specific ventilation is only a fraction of the cost of a centralized system. A further advantage is that the cleaning and maintenance of a supply air device is considerably easier than a conventional supply air duct.

Features

Room-specific supply air solution

Mobair supply air valves are designed to solve room-specific ventilation problems affordably and efficiently. Energy consumption is the lowest in a room-specific ventilation.

Designed for colder climates

Supply air valves are required to bring supply air into the house or a building. Even during the cold winter times, pre-heating Mobair supply air valves can bring the supply air inside without the feeling of draught, with good filtration and very low energy consumption.
