Mobair® 2030S

A fan powered supply air valve for buildings with either natural or mechanical ventilation.

Killer features

Fan creates an efficient airflow

A quiet fan that has an energy consumption of only 1.0 watt, mixes warm indoor air together with the cold outside air and directs the airflow towards the ceiling.

Supply air filtration

The product includes a bag filter of class M5 / ePM10 60% which is installed inside of the fresh air duct.
